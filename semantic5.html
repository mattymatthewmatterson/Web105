Uncommon Ska Influences

One popular style that roots from Ska is known as Dub or Dub Step. Dub is a slower form of Rock Steady and it has inspired many styles of music including Jungle.

Early Jungle beats and record scratching hits featured Dub music. Many "Junglists" today believe that their music wouldn't have existed if it hadn't been for Dub.

Influences of Ska

Reggae

Much more popular than Ska, Reggae has deep political roots. It is still very popular today and even modern groups have Reggae influences

Rock Steady

Originating close to the same time as Reggae, Rock Steady is a type of Ska with more Jazz as well as Rythem and Blues Many people prefer to dance to Rock Steady than Reggae.

Dub Music

Sometimes referred to Dub, Dub descended from Ska through Reggae. Similar to Reggae with it's slower sounds, Dub focuses more on the drums as well as the bass of the music.
